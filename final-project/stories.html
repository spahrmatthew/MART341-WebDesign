We are all storytellers
I believe that we're all storytellers in some way. No matter the format; video, writing, painting, photo, we're telling a story through the content we create. In everything we create, we share a little piece of our mind. Why I think storytelling and stepping out of your comfort zone goes hand in hand is partially a documentarian approach and it is difficult sometimes share what we create. So, I think that it is reasonable to not want to share everything we create, but I do believe that we all have some story to contribute, and you never know how you may make an impact on someone else.
