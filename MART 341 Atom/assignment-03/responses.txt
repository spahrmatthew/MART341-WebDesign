What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?
An IP (Internet Protocol) address is a unique number for a device (similar to an address) where the DNS (Domain Name System) associates name with an IP address through a translative process.

What is a cookie?
A cookie is a file that is left on a device to collect browsing data, remember your device (on the server), and your preferences.

What are the three essential parts of a URL?
The three essential parts of a URL are (a)the protocol, (b) the domain, and (c)paths. These parts all help users navigate to the EXACT location of the site (similar to giving directions for your home).
